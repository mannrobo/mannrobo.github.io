Mann Robotics is a competitive robotics organization at J.L. Mann High School, in Greenville, SC. We compete in the VEX Robotics Competition, and have done so for the past 5 years. During that time, our program has taught planning, teamwork, professional and STEM skills to over 50 students and alumni.

Currently, we have four teams (3796A, 3796B, 3796C, and 3796E, brand new for Turning Point!)
