Don't Forget to Breathe is going to the 2018 VEX World Championships! They have won the Design Award for South Carolina, which "is presented to a team that demonstrates an organized and professional approach to the design process, project and time management and team organization." We're very proud of our newest team for their success!

The 2018 VEX World Championships will be held in Louisville, KY on April 25-28th, 2018.
